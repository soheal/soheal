Special Issue on "Health of Software Ecosystems"

SoHeal 2021 features a Special Issue on “Health of Software Ecosystems” in Science of Computer Programming.

The special issue invites submissions on new and innovative research results and industrial experience papers in the area of software health for software ecosystems that deal with all the aspects of the problem described in the call for the papers of the workshop.

Important dates

  • Submission Deadline: 30th September 2021.
  • Initial Author Notification: 18th January 2022.
  • Initial Author Revision Due: 19th April 2022.
  • Final Author Notification: 19th July 2022.
  • Final Manuscript Submission: 2nd July 2022.
